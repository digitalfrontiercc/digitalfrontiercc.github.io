Spaces & Places

the Netherlands:

varia.zone (Rotterdam) - a space for developing collective approaches to everyday technology.

.zip (Rotterdam) - artist-run project space, organizing events, exhibitions, workshops, test sessions and tool jams.

Creative Coding Utrecht (Utrecht) - a platform for digital creativity. CCU supports creators, connects organizations and transcends sectors, inspires audiences and shares knowledge with hobbyists and ICT professionals.

Waag Futurelab (Amsterdam) - Waag reinforces critical reflection on technology, develops technological and social design skills, and encourages social innovation.

Japan

Civic Creative Base Tokyo - a hub where the public can explore their creative imaginations in society through the use of digital technology.

FabCafe (Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Hida, Nagoya, Fuji) - a series of ‘Fab’ innovation labs that specialize in creating products, services and experiences of the future.
